Cuba Libre - an absolute classic longdrink

The Cuba Libre, English Free Cuba, is a simple, but at the same time sophisticated cocktail with a caffeine kick for long nights! After winning the Spanish-American War, American soldiers toasted their victory with this long drink based on rum, cola and lime. When The Andrews Sisters landed a hit called Rum and Coca Cola after World War II, the Cuba Libre also found its way into Europe and is still an extremely popular cocktail, which should not be missing from any well-assorted drinks menu.

Ingredients

  • For 1 Glass
  • 4 cl Cuban Rum
  • 1 cl lime juice
  • 12 cl Coke
  • Ice cubes

Preparation

5 Minutes

very easy

  1. Pour the Cuban Rum into the glass and add the lime juice
  2. Add the Coke and stirr well
  3. Fill up the glass with ice cubes
